# Prompt Template — Daily Briefing

Copy this template and fill in the brackets, then send it to Copilot.

```
[ACTION]: <Morning, end-of-day, missed (after time away), custom>


OPTIONAL:
- Scope filters: <Calendar only, email only, Teams only, or any combination>
- Priority filters: <Specific people (leadership, manager) or projects>
- Output format: <Inline summary, sent as email, sent as Teams DM>
- Length: <One-line summary, full brief, exec summary + details>
```

## Worked example
Give me a morning briefing — today's meetings, unread emails from leadership, and unanswered Teams DMs.
