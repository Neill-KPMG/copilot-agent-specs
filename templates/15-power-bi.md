# Prompt Template — Power BI

Copy this template and fill in the brackets, then send it to Copilot.

```
[ACTION]: <Report name, dataset name, or Power BI URL>
[QUESTION OR METRIC]: <The specific number, trend, or comparison you want>

OPTIONAL:
- Filters: <Time period, region, product, segment, etc.>
- Output format: <Single number, table, trend line, bar chart, dashboard>
- Aggregation level: <Daily, monthly, quarterly, yearly>
- Comparison baseline: <vs prior period, vs plan, vs same quarter last year>
```

## Worked example
From the Sales Performance report, show total revenue by region for Q1 2026 as a bar chart.
