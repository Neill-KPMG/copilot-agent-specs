# Prompt Template — People & Org

Copy this template and fill in the brackets, then send it to Copilot.

```
[ACTION]: <Name, email, or alias of the person>
[INFO NEEDED]: <Role, manager, direct reports, contact info, all of the above>

OPTIONAL:
- Depth: <Direct reports only, full reporting chain, peers as well>
- Cross-reference: <Recent emails, shared projects, meetings together>
- Output format: <Inline summary, table, org chart-style list>
```

## Worked example
Who is Jamie Chen — role, team, manager, location? And list their direct reports.
