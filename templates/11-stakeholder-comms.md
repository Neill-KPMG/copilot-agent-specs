# Prompt Template — Stakeholder Communications

Copy this template and fill in the brackets, then send it to Copilot.

```
[ACTION]: <Exec update, announcement, escalation, status, cross-functional update>
[AUDIENCE]: <Specific group — leadership team, all-hands, customer success>
[KEY POINTS]: <Bullet list of facts and messages to convey>

OPTIONAL:
- Tone: <Direct, reassuring, urgent, celebratory, etc.>
- Channel: <Email, Teams post, document, or recommendation>
- Length: <One-paragraph, three-bullet, full memo>
- Call to action: <What should the reader do after reading>
- Supporting data: <Specific numbers, quotes, links to include>
```

## Worked example
Draft a risk escalation email to the VP of Engineering about the database migration delay — 3 bullets covering impact, mitigation, and ask — direct tone.
