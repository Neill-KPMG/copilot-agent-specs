# Prompt Template — Calendar Management

Copy this template and fill in the brackets, then send it to Copilot.

```
[ACTION]: <Review / clean up / defend / prioritize / bulk respond>
[TIMEFRAME]: <This week, next week, today, custom range>

OPTIONAL:
- Criteria: <Optional meetings, recurring meetings, low-priority topics>
- Focus time targets: <Number of hours, preferred days/times>
- Auto-decline rules: <What kinds of meetings to auto-decline>
- Conflict resolution: <Which to keep when two events overlap>
```

## Worked example
Review next week's calendar, flag optional meetings I could decline, and protect 2 hours of focus time on Wednesday and Friday.
