# Prompt Template — Deep Research

Copy this template and fill in the brackets, then send it to Copilot.

```
[ACTION]: <What to research>
[SPECIFIC QUESTION OR ANGLE]: <The precise question to answer>

OPTIONAL:
- Time window: <e.g. last 12 months, since 2024, recent quarter>
- Source preference: <Industry reports, news, academic, primary sources>
- Output length: <One-page brief, full report, executive summary>
- Citation style: <Inline links, footnotes, bibliography>
- Comparison points: <Other things to compare or benchmark against>
- Counter-arguments: <Required yes/no — include opposing viewpoints>
```

## Worked example
Research enterprise AI adoption trends in the last 12 months; focus on Fortune 500 case studies; cite all sources.
