# Prompt Template — Calendar — Scheduling

Copy this template and fill in the brackets, then send it to Copilot.

```
[ACTION]: <Create, update, cancel, reschedule, accept, decline>
[ATTENDEES]: <Names or email addresses — include all required participants>
[DURATION]: <e.g. 30 min, 1 hour, 90 min>
[TIME WINDOW]: <e.g. tomorrow morning, next Tue or Wed afternoon>

OPTIONAL:
- Subject / title: <Short descriptive title for the event>
- Location or room: <Specific room, address, or 'find a room'>
- Online meeting: <Teams meeting yes/no (default: yes)>
- Recurrence: <Daily, weekly, every-other-week, monthly, etc.>
- Constraints: <Avoid focus blocks, respect time zones, working hours only>
- Description / agenda: <Body of the invite>
```

## Worked example
Schedule a 45-min sync with Alex and Priya next Tuesday or Wednesday afternoon, Teams meeting, avoid my focus blocks.
