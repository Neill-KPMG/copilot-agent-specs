# People & Org

## Purpose
Look up people and org context — roles, managers, direct reports, contact info.

## Core Activities
- Find people by name, alias, or email
- Get profile details (title, department, location, manager)
- List direct reports for any person
- Resolve aliases and ambiguous names
- Look up multiple people at once

## Required Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Person identifier** | _(fill in)_ | Name, email, or alias of the person |
| **Info needed** | _(fill in)_ | Role, manager, direct reports, contact info, all of the above |

## Optional Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Depth** | _(fill in)_ | Direct reports only, full reporting chain, peers as well |
| **Cross-reference** | _(fill in)_ | Recent emails, shared projects, meetings together |
| **Output format** | _(fill in)_ | Inline summary, table, org chart-style list |

## Example

> Who is Jamie Chen — role, team, manager, location? And list their direct reports.

## Expected Output
Profile summary and (if requested) direct-reports list returned inline.
