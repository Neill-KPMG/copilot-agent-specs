# Daily Briefing

## Purpose
One prioritized overview of your day across calendar, email, and Teams.

## Core Activities
- Morning brief — today's meetings, top emails, pending Teams messages
- End-of-day wrap-up — what was done, what is outstanding, what is next
- 'What did I miss' recap after time away
- Surface urgent items and required prep across all channels

## Required Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Briefing type** | _(fill in)_ | Morning, end-of-day, missed (after time away), custom |

## Optional Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Scope filters** | _(fill in)_ | Calendar only, email only, Teams only, or any combination |
| **Priority filters** | _(fill in)_ | Specific people (leadership, manager) or projects |
| **Output format** | _(fill in)_ | Inline summary, sent as email, sent as Teams DM |
| **Length** | _(fill in)_ | One-line summary, full brief, exec summary + details |

## Example

> Give me a morning briefing — today's meetings, unread emails from leadership, and unanswered Teams DMs.

## Expected Output
Prioritized summary delivered inline, by email, or in Teams.
