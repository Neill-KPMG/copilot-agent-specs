# Calendar Management

## Purpose
Strategic calendar health — review, classify events, defend focus time, resolve conflicts.

## Core Activities
- Review calendar density and classify events by purpose
- Identify low-value or optional meetings to decline
- Defend focus time and protect deep-work blocks
- Resolve scheduling conflicts and propose alternatives
- Bulk accept, decline, or tentatively accept invitations

## Required Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Goal** | _(fill in)_ | Review / clean up / defend / prioritize / bulk respond |
| **Timeframe** | _(fill in)_ | This week, next week, today, custom range |

## Optional Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Criteria** | _(fill in)_ | Optional meetings, recurring meetings, low-priority topics |
| **Focus time targets** | _(fill in)_ | Number of hours, preferred days/times |
| **Auto-decline rules** | _(fill in)_ | What kinds of meetings to auto-decline |
| **Conflict resolution** | _(fill in)_ | Which to keep when two events overlap |

## Example

> Review next week's calendar, flag optional meetings I could decline, and protect 2 hours of focus time on Wednesday and Friday.

## Expected Output
Recommendations and (with approval) calendar changes applied.
