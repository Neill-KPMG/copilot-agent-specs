# Calendar — Scheduling

## Purpose
Create, modify, or cancel calendar events; find times that work across attendees; book rooms.

## Core Activities
- Create, update, and cancel events (single occurrence or full series)
- Accept, decline, or tentatively accept invitations
- Find meeting times across multiple attendees and check free/busy
- Book conference rooms from your tenant directory
- Forward events to additional people as FYI notifications
- Reschedule, add or remove attendees, and update event details

## Required Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Action** | _(fill in)_ | Create, update, cancel, reschedule, accept, decline |
| **Attendees** | _(fill in)_ | Names or email addresses — include all required participants |
| **Duration** | _(fill in)_ | e.g. 30 min, 1 hour, 90 min |
| **Time window** | _(fill in)_ | e.g. tomorrow morning, next Tue or Wed afternoon |

## Optional Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Subject / title** | _(fill in)_ | Short descriptive title for the event |
| **Location or room** | _(fill in)_ | Specific room, address, or 'find a room' |
| **Online meeting** | _(fill in)_ | Teams meeting yes/no (default: yes) |
| **Recurrence** | _(fill in)_ | Daily, weekly, every-other-week, monthly, etc. |
| **Constraints** | _(fill in)_ | Avoid focus blocks, respect time zones, working hours only |
| **Description / agenda** | _(fill in)_ | Body of the invite |

## Example

> Schedule a 45-min sync with Alex and Priya next Tuesday or Wednesday afternoon, Teams meeting, avoid my focus blocks.

## Expected Output
Calendar event created (or updated/cancelled), invitations sent to attendees.
