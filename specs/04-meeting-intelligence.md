# Meeting Intelligence

## Purpose
Prep for upcoming meetings, summarize past ones, and extract decisions and action items.

## Core Activities
- Pull transcripts from Teams meetings (most recent or specific)
- Summarize what happened in a meeting (key points, decisions, action items)
- Identify attendees, speakers, and time spent on topics
- Prepare briefs for upcoming meetings (agenda, attendee context, related emails/files)
- Recap 'what did I miss' for meetings you skipped

## Required Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Action** | _(fill in)_ | Prep / summarize / extract / recap |
| **Meeting reference** | _(fill in)_ | Meeting title, date/time, or 'most recent' |

## Optional Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Output focus** | _(fill in)_ | Decisions only, action items only, attendee insights, key quotes |
| **Time range** | _(fill in)_ | If scanning multiple meetings |
| **Related context** | _(fill in)_ | Pull in related emails, files, or prior meeting notes |
| **Audience for brief** | _(fill in)_ | Who will read the brief — adjusts level of detail |

## Example

> Prep me for tomorrow's 9am budget review — pull the last meeting's action items, attendee roles, and related emails from finance.

## Expected Output
Inline summary or brief, optionally delivered as an email or saved as a document.
