# Deep Research

## Purpose
Source-backed investigation with citations for verifiable claims.

## Core Activities
- Multi-source web research with full source citation
- Fact-checking and verification of specific claims
- Literature reviews and competitive analyses
- Synthesis of findings across many sources
- Trustworthy claims with full traceability

## Required Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Topic** | _(fill in)_ | What to research |
| **Specific question or angle** | _(fill in)_ | The precise question to answer |

## Optional Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Time window** | _(fill in)_ | e.g. last 12 months, since 2024, recent quarter |
| **Source preference** | _(fill in)_ | Industry reports, news, academic, primary sources |
| **Output length** | _(fill in)_ | One-page brief, full report, executive summary |
| **Citation style** | _(fill in)_ | Inline links, footnotes, bibliography |
| **Comparison points** | _(fill in)_ | Other things to compare or benchmark against |
| **Counter-arguments** | _(fill in)_ | Required yes/no — include opposing viewpoints |

## Example

> Research enterprise AI adoption trends in the last 12 months; focus on Fortune 500 case studies; cite all sources.

## Expected Output
Written report with verifiable citations, returned inline or as a document.
