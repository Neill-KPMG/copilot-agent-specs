# Power BI

## Purpose
Query published Power BI reports and semantic models for metrics and trends.

## Core Activities
- Find Power BI reports and datasets by keyword
- Inspect report pages, visuals, and filters
- Explore semantic-model schema (tables, measures, relationships)
- Run DAX queries for specific metrics and slices
- Analyze trends, comparisons, KPIs, and render as charts

## Required Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Report or dataset reference** | _(fill in)_ | Report name, dataset name, or Power BI URL |
| **Question or metric** | _(fill in)_ | The specific number, trend, or comparison you want |

## Optional Fields

| Field | Your Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Filters** | _(fill in)_ | Time period, region, product, segment, etc. |
| **Output format** | _(fill in)_ | Single number, table, trend line, bar chart, dashboard |
| **Aggregation level** | _(fill in)_ | Daily, monthly, quarterly, yearly |
| **Comparison baseline** | _(fill in)_ | vs prior period, vs plan, vs same quarter last year |

## Example

> From the Sales Performance report, show total revenue by region for Q1 2026 as a bar chart.

## Expected Output
Tabular result or rendered chart returned inline.
